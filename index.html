Welcome to my homepage.

My name is Christos Moyzes (Χρήστος Μωυζές). I was born in 1986 in Athens, Greece.
I am a post-doctoral researcher at Nova University Lisbon (Faculty of Science and Technology) for project FORGET;
the project is lead by João Leite.
I received my PhD from the Computer Science Department of the University of Liverpool;
my primary supervisor was Wiebe van der Hoek and my secondary supervisor was Davide Grossi.
I am a graduate of MPLA; thesis was supervised by Costas D. Koutras.
I also hold a Mathematics degree from the University of Athens.

Research Interests

Modal Logics – Epistemic/Doxastic and Dynamic Logics, Model Theory and Proof Systems of Modal Logics.

Computational Logic – Logic-based Knowledge Representation, Belief Revision, Non-monotonic Logics.

Artificial Intelligence – Mathematical Logic and Automated Theorem Proving.
